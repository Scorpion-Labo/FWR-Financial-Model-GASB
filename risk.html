Contingency triggers

Activate Plan B if attendance < 85% of plan by month 6, or membership sales < 60% of target by month 9 — pause Phase-2 build, cut robot adds to ~$1.0M/yr, trim discretionary spend, hold headcount.

Activate Plan C if attendance < 65% by month 9, or cumulative cash falls below a 6-month opex reserve — sublease the unbuilt 12,000 sq ft, hiring freeze, marketing to maintenance, core streams only.

Re-upgrade (C→B→A) when attendance recovers above the next tier for two consecutive quarters.

Cash note

  • Plan B Yr-1 cumulative cash ≈ −$9.9M
  • Plan C Yr-1 cumulative cash ≈ −$7.2M
  • Year-1 build cost is fixed while revenue is lower
  • The raise is sized to cover this; contingency protects Years 2+
