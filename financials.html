Basis of presentation — governmental enterprise fund (GASB)

FutureWorld Robotics is presented as an enterprise fund (business-type activity) on the economic-resources measurement focus and the accrual basis of accounting, in conformity with U.S. GAAP as prescribed by the Governmental Accounting Standards Board (GASB). Net position is reported in three components (net investment in capital assets, restricted, unrestricted); revenues and expenses are separated into operating and nonoperating; and the statement of cash flows uses the direct method with four categories plus the required reconciliation of operating income to net cash from operating activities. As a governmental entity the center is tax-exempt, and the initial capital is reported as a capital contribution rather than equity. This is an illustrative recast of the underlying five-year model.
