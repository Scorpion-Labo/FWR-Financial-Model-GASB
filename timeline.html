Implementation Timeline

Public-safe

A fast-track plan: every workstream runs in parallel from financing close to a Christmas 2026 soft opening and a Q1 2027 grand opening.

The objective is a soft opening about six months from financing close. Sourcing is a core strength: established China manufacturing partners deliver the celebrity androids and supporting robots on an accelerated schedule, removing the long-lead bottleneck that would otherwise constrain a launch of this kind.

What makes Christmas 2026 achievable

Immediate financing close with same-week robot orders; a proven China supply chain delivering the full fleet; a near-turnkey leased space; and fully parallel execution. The phased build — opening the first 24,000 sq ft first — gives the schedule built-in flexibility.
